About Us

The brain is the organ of destiny. It holds within its humming mechanism secrets that will determine the future of the human race.
We plan to scale outward, starting with childhood epilepsy and eventually ending epilepsy for everyone.

We founded Loop AI with the vision of maximizing the potential of the human brain.

Our brains process several billions of bits of data daily—we could be doing so much with all that power, but we can't until we unlock the full potential of our cognition. At Loop, we want to optimize what humans are focusing on to increase productivity and tap into that incredible machine that is our brain.
